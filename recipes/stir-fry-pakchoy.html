Simple and Easy Stir Fry Pakchoy Recipe

Description:

Here is simple way to cook pakchoy. Pokchoy has other names; bokchoy, pokchoy,etc.

Ingredients:

  • Around 5 bunches of pakchoy/bokchoy
  • Ginger as you wish
  • Garlic as you wish
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Cooking Oil

Steps:

  1. Make a grounded garlic
  2. Chop the ginger into slices
  3. Heat a wok or a pan
  4. Then pour pokchoy into it with nothing
  5. Stir it until couple minutes, then take them out.
  6. Now pour the cooking oil into the pan/wok, and cook the grounded garlics and sliced of gingers
  7. Pour pakchoy, stir it then add Oyster Sauce
  8. Stir fry couple minutes, then done!
