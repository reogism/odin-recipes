Simple and Easy Steamed Eggplants

Description:

One of easiest eggplant recipe with simple ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggplants standard size
  • Half glass of water
  • 1 spoon of Fish Sauce
  • 2 spoon of Soy Souce

You can add more ingredients, such as spring onions, chilli paste, etc.

Steps:

  1. Cut the eggplants into three parts
  2. Slice each part into half
  3. Pour water into your steamer pan
  4. Heat the water until it boils
  5. Put eggplants in steamer and cover with steamer lid
  6. Wait 5 minutes, try to porking eggplants. If it is soft, then it done. Make sure the eggplants not to soft and not to hard.
  7. Let it cool, then shred eggplants to make smaller pieces.
  8. Pour the Fish sauce and Soy sauce on shredded eggsplants, then stir by hand or spoon until the sauce is evenly mixed.
  9. You can add it with another ingredients, such as spring onions, and chilli paste as you wish.
